Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

Do’s 
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible 
- Verify website URLs before entering personal or banking details 
- Keep your devices and apps updated regularly 
- Use secure and trusted Wi-Fi networks only 
- Regularly check bank statements and transaction history 
- Install apps only from official sources (Play Store/App Store) 
- Immediately report suspicious activity on cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930

Don’ts 
- Do not share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone 
- Do not click on unknown links or suspicious messages 
- Do not trust calls claiming to be from banks asking for details
- Do not save sensitive information on public/shared devices 
- Do not use public Wi-Fi for financial transactions 
- Do not download attachments from unknown emails 
- Do not ignore unusual account activity or alerts 
- Do not respond to fake job offers or prize messages

UPI / Financial Safety Tips 
- Always verify the receiver’s name before making payment 
- Never approve unknown UPI collect requests 
- Do not scan random QR codes for receiving money 
- Use official banking apps only for transactions

Report Cybercrime National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline Number: 1930
