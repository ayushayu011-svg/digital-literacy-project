# Social Media Responsibility Checklist (College Student)

## ✅ Do's
- Think before posting – ensure your content reflects your values and future goals.
- Maintain privacy – limit sharing personal details like address, phone number, or routine.
- Use professional language – especially on platforms like LinkedIn.
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
- Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely in discussions.

## ❌ Don'ts
- Don’t post offensive, abusive, or discriminatory content.
- Don’t share fake news or unverified information.
- Don’t overshare personal life or sensitive information.
- Don’t engage in online arguments or toxic debates.
- Don’t damage your digital reputation with inappropriate photos or comments.
